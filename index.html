宮古島の素材を、実直に引き出す。

Drawing out the true essence of Miyakojima's ingredients.

島が育む豊かな食材を、日本料理の確かな技法で仕立てます。丁寧にひいた出汁、一貫ずつ握る寿司、職人が自ら打つ締めの手打ち蕎麦。琉球石灰岩の層に磨かれた宮古島の清らかな地下水に敬意を払い、枠にとらわれない一期一会の一皿をお愉しみください。

We prepare the island's abundant ingredients using reliable Japanese culinary techniques. Savor our carefully brewed dashi, hand-pressed sushi, and handmade buckwheat soba to finish. Paying tribute to Miyakojima's pure underground water filtered through Ryukyu limestone, we welcome you to enjoy a once-in-a-lifetime dining experience.
